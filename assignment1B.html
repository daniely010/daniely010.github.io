The rules of flag football

Basic rules:

  • No tackling: Instead of tackling, players pull a flag from the belt of whoever has the ball
  • 5 players on the field: The game is usually played with smaller teams with less people than traditional football
  • First downs: Teams must advance the ball a certain distance (around half the field) to regain first down
  • QB: Quarterbacks are not allowed forward movement or running unless it's a direct handoff
Introduction to football: Know the basics

Scoring

  1. Touchdown: Worth 6 points
  2. Conversion: After a touchdown, teams can earn 1 or 2 points for 1 extra play
  3. Safety: Worth 2 points if a down occurs in the offense's endzone
